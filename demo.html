DONT DELAY YOUR TREATMENTS

Risks of Delayed treatment

  • Minor problems become major problems
  • Recovery time increases
  • Quality of life declines
  • Compensatory/New problem develop

Benifits of treatment

  • Return to physical activity
  • Rejoin family Activity
  • Reduction in Pain
  • Quality of life improves

For individuals with an ACL tear a chance of a meniscal tear increases by 2%
with each week of delay !.
